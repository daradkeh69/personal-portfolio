I'm deeply interested in cybersecurity, ethical hacking, and competitive Capture The Flag (CTF) challenges—especially in areas like OSINT and web exploitation. I've already made strong progress on platforms like Hack The Box, and Participated in the National Cyber Security Competition (NCSC).

I'm also someone who thinks long-term. I've got a vision for financial independence and building a better future, and I'm always exploring ways to grow—whether it's by learning something new, entering competitions, or thinking about how to start my own cybersecurity company.
