About Me

Hello! I'm Adam Daradkeh, a passionate IT student studying BTEC IT in Jordan. I'm deeply interested in cybersecurity, ethical hacking, and competitive Capture The Flag (CTF) challenges.

I specialize in OSINT and web exploitation, with strong progress on platforms like Hack The Box. I've qualified for the National Cyber Security Competition finals in Jordan (NCSC), demonstrating my commitment to excellence in cybersecurity.

My goal is to build a cybersecurity business, offering penetration testing and vulnerability assessments to help organizations secure their digital presence. I believe in continuous learning and staying ahead of emerging threats.
